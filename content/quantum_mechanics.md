Title: The Origins of Quantum Mechanics: A Journey into the Subatomic World
Date: 2024-10-25 10:00
Category: Science

The origins of quantum mechanics date back to the early 20th century. This field of physics revolutionized our understanding of nature at the molecular, atomic, and subatomic levels.

It all started when Max Planck proposed that energy is emitted or absorbed in discrete "quanta" to explain blackbody radiation. Later, Albert Einstein expanded on this with the photoelectric effect, showing light itself behaves both as particles and waves. This duality is central to the mystery of the subatomic domain.
