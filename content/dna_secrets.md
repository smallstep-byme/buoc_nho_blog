Title: Unraveling the Secrets of DNA: The Molecule of Life
Date: 2024-10-24 10:00
Category: Biology

Deoxyribonucleic acid, commonly known as DNA, is the fundamental building block of life as we know it. Its discovery has paved the way for modern genetics, medicine, and evolutionary biology.

The double helix structure of DNA, described by James Watson and Francis Crick (with crucial data from Rosalind Franklin), revealed how genetic information is copied and passed on from generation to generation.
